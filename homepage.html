New United Way Branding

United Way Worldwide has updated their branding standards, and so have we! We're really happy to have worked directly with UWW to develop a new Look and Feel Template to install for all your eCommunity webpages. Documentation to add a new template is in our support center, and all instructions for every template, including any necessary coding, are built right into the template builder itself.

New Dashboard Installer

Dashboards and Biotabs are incredibly powerful tools. To make it even easier to get everything you need at your fingertips, we've added the ability to install Dashboards and Biotabs right from your Andar 360 application. You can use the link below, or just navigate to the Biotabs and Dashboards Maintenance menu item and click the "Install from Andar" button.

Tribute Gifts

Does your site use a survey to gather "in honor of" or "in memory of" pledges? If so, we've got exciting news! We have implemented Tribute Gifts, which allows your donors to automatically create a gift relationship, while they're pledging, directly in ePledge. Adding a Tribute Gift will create the appropriate gift relationship, and allow you to match it to an existing account in Andar.

e-Volunteer Enhancements

We've been busy enhancing our Volunteer Management and e-Volunteer module! Some of the enhancements included in this Service Pack include: automated volunteer hour assignment, a new mechanism for tracking the value of volunteer hours, self-reporting of hours for opportunities not in Andar, a host of new dashboard apps, and reporting improvements.
