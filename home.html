What's New in 2025.01

Brand new in the 2025.01 service pack, we've introduced this "What's New" splash screen. We know release notes are no fun to read, and not everyone in the organization gets them. Why not show you all the great things our customers have asked for, and our developers have delivered, right here. In the last year, we've completed over 275 enhancement projects and feature requests, and now, with each new service pack, you can expect to see some highlights of those new features, modules, enhancements, Dashboards, and Biotabs as soon as you update.

New United Way Branding

United Way Worldwide has updated their branding standards, and so have we! We're really happy to have worked directly with UWW to develop a new Look and Feel Template to install for all your eCommunity webpages. Documentation to add a new template is in our support center, and all instructions for every template, including any necessary coding, are built right into the template builder itself.

New Dashboard Installer

Dashboards and Biotabs are incredibly powerful tools to help group all the important data, right in one screen. We have been releasing new Dashboards and Biotabs to our support site for a few years now, but to make it even easier to get everything you need at your fingertips, we've added the ability to install Dashboards and Biotabs right from your Andar 360 application.

Tribute Gifts

Does your site use a survey to gather "in honor of" or "in memory of" pledges on your ePledge pages? If so, we've got exciting news! We have implemented Tribute Gifts, which allows your donors to automatically create a gift relationship, while they're pledging, directly in ePledge.

e-Volunteer Enhancements

We've been busy enhancing our Volunteer Management and e-Volunteer module! Some of the enhancements included in this Service Pack include: automated volunteer hour assignment, hour assignment via e-Volunteer, a new mechanism for tracking the value of volunteer hours, self-reporting of hours for opportunities not in Andar, a host of new dashboard apps, reporting improvements, and a better in app experience and web workflows.

eVolunteer Snapshot Dashboard

If you're a current or prospective e-Volunteer module owner, we have released a whole host of new Dashboard apps, all geared to help you understand your volunteers' behaviors and monitor your volunteer opportunities. The Snapshot - e-Volunteer Dashboard pulls together information about how many volunteer opportunities need filled, some history on popular volunteering opportunities, and what the value of your volunteering has been.
